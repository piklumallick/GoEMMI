Frequently Asked Questions

General

How do I get to Göttingen?

Göttingen is located in central Germany and is well connected by the Deutsche Bahn high-speed rail system. Book tickets for Göttingen Central Station (Bahnhof).

From Frankfurt Airport (FRA), you can reach Göttingen in ~2h via high-speed rail from the Frankfurt Flughafen Fernbahnhof located in Terminal 1.

Where will the GoEMMI school be held?

Hosted by the Max Planck Institute for Dynamics and Self-Organization at Am Faßberg. Take bus lines 21, 22, or 23 from the city center/station (approx. 25 mins).

Click here for more information.

Who is the GoEMMI winter school for?

It is intended for PhD students in computational neuroscience, statistical physics, complex dynamics, information theory, and biological/machine learning.

What is the format of the school?

A mixture of theoretical lectures and hands-on tutorials. Students will also complete an independent project presented on the final day.

Where can I stay?

Students will be accommodated at the Max Planck Institute Guest house. Lecturers may book their own accommodations for reimbursement.
